# Incident Response Notes

These are my beginner notes on the incident response process.

## What is Incident Response?
Incident response is the process of identifying, managing, containing, and recovering from security incidents.

## Common Incident Response Phases
1. Preparation
2. Detection and Analysis
3. Containment
4. Eradication
5. Recovery
6. Lessons Learned

## Why Incident Response Matters
A good incident response process helps reduce damage, restore systems faster, and improve security after an incident.

## My Learning Goal
I am studying incident response to better understand how security teams investigate and respond to threats.
